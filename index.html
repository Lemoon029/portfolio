Trigger Catalogue

Introduction

This is a fan-made website by a fan of the series World Trigger. The goal of this site is to give a simple and quick way for other World Trigger fans to overview all of the currently known Triggers. As of now, this site only covers most of the main offensive triggers of the 3 main factions: Border, Aftokrator, and Galopoula. Hopefully, someday the scope of this site will expand to cover all of the sub-triggers as well as even trion soldiers.

Border Triggers

Triggers used by the organization of Border on Earth. Border triggers are different from most typical neighbor triggers as they are custom constructed with an arrangement of multiple main and sub-trigger abilities, rather than based around one main trigger function such as black triggers. As such, a physical trigger carried by a Border Agent is likely to have multiple of the trigger functions listed below.

Each trigger function is also mass-produced and designed to be usable by people of different skill level, unlike specialized neighbor triggers that require specific training for an individual to use their trigger.
Kogestu: The first attacker trigger developed by boarder. It appears as a katana. It has excellent durability and perhaps even better cutting power. Despite being fairly heavy, it is a pretty balanced trigger.
Scorpion: The second developed attacker trigger. Often appearing as a glowing blade, scorpion can actually shapeshift into many different forms. It can be quickly formed and deformed from any part of the users body as well. As a result, it is not very sturdy. One of the most versatile triggers out there.
Raygust: A third attack trigger. Has two forms: a sword form and a shield form. It can freely transform between these forms making it the most defensive attack trigger. The shield mode of the raygust is much stronger than the ordinary shield trigger used by all agents. Raygust is quite heavy.
Sogetsu: An attacker trigger that is designed to typically be duel wielded. They seem to be fairly light and can be quite powerful. Despite this, they are not commonly used. This may be because of their shorter range making them less reliable than a Kogestsu and less versatility than a Scorpion or Raygust.
Boarder has many more triggers than what is listed. Unfortunately, those triggers are outside the scope of this project (at this point in time anyways).

Aftokrator Triggers

Aftokrator is commonly seen as the most powerful nation in the Neighborhood. It is also referred to as "the Holy Land". Their triggers take advantage of horn technology that enchances the trion of their soldiers. As a result their triggers tend to be much more powerful than typical triggers.

Lampyris: While not active appears like a black metal crown. While active, it allows the user to control many metallic-like shards. They have controllable magentic and electric properties. The shards can also be precisely used to reflect many projectile triggers. The shards can even be used as trackers. A very versatile trigger.
Chelidon: When active, it takes the form of a giant red gun that can modify its shape and firing style. In addition to the gun, this trigger also serves as a jetback like device that allows the user to fly. The power and flexibility of this shooter trigger makes it extrememly powerful.
Aftokrator has more Black Triggers than any other known Nation. There are currently 4 known ones; however, Aftokrator is known to have had 13 in the past, and may now possess even more than that! The next 4 triggers are the currently known Black Triggers.
Alektor: Appears as a translucent egg that summons and controls many types of translucent animals. Each animal moves in a distinct way. Uniquely, these animals do not cause damage like the conventional weapon trigger. Instead the animals convert other trion into basic cubes on contact (including trion bodies). These animals have no effect on non-trion substances but some types are able to go through them.
Speiraskia: Appears as a purplish black hole the size of a hand. Allows the user to create portals (that look the same) that can lead to other locations. The exact limits and rules of where and how far portals can be placed are currently unknown, but it is fairly versatile. The user can also create portals that summon spikes that quickly stab outwards.
Borboros: Also known as vorvoros. Borboros allows the user to turn their trion body into any form and shape, including liguid and gas. This can be used in many ways including offensively as the user can turn parts of their body into deadly spikes. Any damage to the body has near no effect as the users form can simply be put back together again. The user can also harden their body beyond normal. The only weakness the user has is their essential trion organs (but these can be moved any where within the body.
Organon: Considered a national treasure of Aftokrator. Appears like a cane when active; however, it can be opened revealing itself to actually be a concealed sword. It can create giant spinning rings around the user with huge swords attached. These rings are typically impossible to see because they move to quickly for the human eye to track. These ring blades are strong enough to slice through entire buildings without even slowing down.

Galopoula Triggers

Galopoula does not boast as much manpower or triggers as Aftokrator, but it does specialize in small amounts of elite troops. Their triggers are fairly powerful

Vasilissa: A very advanced trigger. Capable of shooting a devastating cannon. Since the cannon is so powerful it requires a long cool down. Each arm of this trigger is very durable.
Despinis: Multiple spinning projectiles. Very fast and sharp. They can be redirected by the user in a moments notice.
Nikokyra: A pink slime trigger. Is very useful for disarming your foe. Seems to be able to take different forms such as sticky or slippery. It is very flexible with how it is controlled as it can float in the air by the user.
Servitora: A trigger that allows the user the create copies of themselves. These are mirages rather than actual clones. This trigger can also change the appearance of the user adding to the potential trickery.
